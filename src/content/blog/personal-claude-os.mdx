Your `~/.claude/` directory is more than a config folder. It is a portable developer system that carries your agent personas, custom commands, and permission rules across every project you touch.

The key insight is that `~/.claude/` is version-controllable. Drop it in a GitHub repo, clone it on a new machine, and your entire agent environment — SOUL.md personas, slash command skills, lifecycle hooks, and permission gates — is ready in seconds.

This is the foundation of what I call a Personal OS: a localized, self-improving cognitive workspace that runs directly on your machine, reading your files, executing tasks, learning your preferences, and compounding your professional intelligence across every session.
