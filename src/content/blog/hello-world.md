Welcome to **DevDiary.uk** — my public engineering diary. This is where I document everything I build, everything I learn, and everything that breaks along the way.

## What This Is

![Developer diary and keyboard setup](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1499750310107-5fef28a66643?w=800)

A developer's diary focused on **building authority through real work**. No abstract theory, no fluff. Every post is rooted in a real build, a real integration, or a real debugging session. I ship first, then write about what I learned.

## What You'll Find Here

![Roadmap or journey visualization](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1522071820081-009f0129c71c?w=800)

- **Claude Code & AI Agent Development** — Custom agents, `~/.claude/` configuration, MCP servers, and agentic workflow patterns. Start with the [10-Minute Quick Start Setup](/posts/claude-code-personal-os-guide/) or dive into the [Complete Mastery Guide](/posts/claude-code-complete-mastery-guide/)
- **AI Productivity Systems** — Personal OS architecture, knowledge compounding, auto-dreaming, and moving from chatbot user to agent operator. See [Build Your AI Chief of Staff](/posts/build-ai-chief-of-staff-claude-code-guide/) for the full blueprint
- **Modern Agentic Systems** — Phase-gated agents, batch-and-specialize patterns, self-learning pipelines. Explore [Arc: The Self-Learning ERP Agent](/posts/building-arc-erp-agent/) and the [MIS v2 LLM Architecture](/posts/mis-v2-llm-agents/)
- **Web Development & Architecture** — Astro, Supabase, Node.js, TypeScript — the real stack decisions behind production systems
- **The opencode Ecosystem** — Custom agents, skills, SOUL.md philosophy, and integration patterns for LLM-powered development

## Who This Is For

Developers who want to move beyond prompting and into operating. If you're already comfortable with a terminal and curious about what happens when you treat AI as an infrastructure layer rather than a chat interface — you're in the right place.
