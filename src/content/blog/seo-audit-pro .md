I've audited dozens of sites over the years. The process is always the same: run five different tools, export the reports, merge them into a spreadsheet, prioritise by hand, then write up the findings.

Every SEO tool on the market works the same way. Free ones give you three generic suggestions and ask you to upgrade. Paid ones cost $50–$200/month and give you data dumps that still require manual interpretation. Hiring an agency starts at $2,000 for a single audit.

There's nothing in between — a tool that walks you through a professional-grade audit, shows you exactly where you stand, and tells you what to fix first. **So I built it.**

![SEO audit dashboard showing scored modules and priority action items on a laptop screen](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1460925895917-afdab827c52f?w=800)

## Why most SEO audits waste your time

The problem with automated SEO tools is that they're promiscuous. They scan everything and report everything, because they don't know who you are or what matters. The result is a 40-page PDF where three things are actually actionable and the rest is noise.

SEO Audit Pro takes the opposite approach. It's a guided manual audit framework — you check what's working, flag what isn't, and the tool scores, prioritises, and generates a report. It forces you to *think* about each check instead of skimming an automated dump.

## What's inside

Ten audit modules that cover every layer of SEO, from crawlability to conversion tracking. Each module has weighted checks with pass/partial/fail scoring, so you get a precise picture of where you stand.

- **Technical SEO** — Crawlability, indexation, URL structure, redirects, SSL
- **On-Page SEO** — Title tags, meta descriptions, heading hierarchy, keyword usage
- **Content Quality** — Depth, originality, E-E-A-T signals, readability
- **Mobile & Core Web Vitals** — Responsive design, LCP, INP, CLS
- **Page Speed** — Server response, image optimization, caching, CDN
- **Structured Data** — Schema.org, JSON-LD, rich snippets
- **Internal Linking** — Link architecture, anchor text, orphan detection
- **Backlink Profile** — Link quality, toxic links, competitor gaps
- **Local SEO** — GBP, NAP consistency, local citations
- **Analytics & Tracking** — GA4, Search Console, conversion goals

### Auto-scored dashboard

Every check you complete updates your score in real time — overall score, per-module breakdowns, and a radar chart visualisation. No waiting for reports to generate.

### Executive summary that writes itself

The tool generates a plain-English executive summary based on your findings — strengths, critical issues, and prioritised recommendations. Edit it, print it, send it to a client.

### Multi-project support

Run audits for multiple clients or sites. Each project saves to your browser (localStorage) — no accounts, no data leaves your machine.
